# 📖 The Unwritten Ode

> **"Every story hides another."**

The Unwritten Ode is a narrative mystery adventure prototype developed for the **Code & Clues Hackathon**.

Players take on the role of **Arjun**, a first-year student who unexpectedly discovers a mysterious book that leads him into a forgotten story hidden within his university. By exploring different locations, interacting with clues, and uncovering evidence, players slowly reveal the truth behind the mystery.

---

## 🎮 Features

- Interactive Main Menu
- Story Introduction
- Room Investigation
- Hall of Fame Exploration
- Evidence Collection
- Narrative-Based Ending
- Atmospheric UI inspired by mystery games

## 🎯 Objective

The project focuses on delivering an engaging narrative experience where players investigate clues rather than relying on combat or action mechanics. The prototype demonstrates the game's atmosphere, storytelling approach, and exploration mechanics.
